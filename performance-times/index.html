Performance Times

National Finals Rodeo is scheduled for December 5-14, 2024 at the Thomas & Mack Center in Las Vegas. The Wrangler NFR is an event you should attend at least once in your life. The Wrangler NFR will return to the Thomas & Mack Center December 5-14, marking the 38th year the Wrangler NFR will be held in Las Vegas.
