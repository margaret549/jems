]]> This Friday morning, on the third day of the 76th Chilean National Rodeo Championship, the Caballos series was held, with 42 teams in the Medialuna Monumental arena.

The division was won by the duo composed of Francisco Moreno and Alfredo Moreno (Santiago Sur Association) in Talero and Remo with 38 good points (11+8+8+11).

Meanwhile, second place went to José Manuel Toledo and Gustavo Ortega (Petorca Association) in Bambino and Satan with 30 (8+8+4+10).

Meanwhile, third place and the subsequent qualification for Sunday’s Champions Series went to the local team of Raúl Contesse and Claudio Bustos (Asociación Río Cachapoal) in Morhuilla and Campo Lindo with 28 (9+3+7+9).

The day will continue with the Yeguas y Potros series.
